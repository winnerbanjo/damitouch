Clear terms before you book.

Review check-in times, cancellations, refunds, payments, and booking expectations.

House rules

  • No smoking indoors. Smoking is allowed outdoors only. Burns on furniture will result in forfeiture of the caution fee.
  • Maximum occupancy is 4 guests.
  • No unauthorized gatherings or parties. All gatherings must be pre-approved.
  • No DJ, external speakers, or external equipment allowed.
  • Keep noise to a minimum and respect estate regulations.
  • Leave the apartment tidy.

Caution fee policy

  • The caution fee will be refunded within 24 hours after check-out, provided no damages or rule violations occur.
  • Smoking indoors, unauthorized gatherings, damages to furniture or fittings, and leaving the apartment excessively dirty may result in full or partial forfeiture.
  • If the cost of damages exceeds the caution fee, the tenant will be liable to pay the remaining balance.

Estate rules

  • No fighting, physical confrontations, aggressive behavior, or violent behavior. Penalty: ₦50,000 fine and immediate termination of stay without refund.
  • No loud shouting or noise pollution outside the compound. No yelling, music, or gatherings outside the building between 9:00 PM and 8:00 AM. Penalty: ₦30,000 fine per incident.
  • No parties or unauthorized gatherings unless pre-approved by management. Penalty: ₦50,000 and possible eviction.
  • No smoking indoors. Smoking is only allowed in designated outdoor areas. Penalty: ₦25,000 cleaning fee if violated.
  • No illegal activities.
